# Discord Integration Documentation

This guide will walk you through the steps to create a Discord bot and integrate it into your server.

[https://www.loom.com/share/88abd3ca9afd456cae957a5fc3e2e60e?sid=f003d6b1-678a-425f-a615-790105797178](https://www.loom.com/share/88abd3ca9afd456cae957a5fc3e2e60e?sid=f003d6b1-678a-425f-a615-790105797178)

## Step 1: Create a Discord Server

1. **Log in to your Discord account.**
2. **Click the add server button** (`+` button) on the left sidebar.
3. **Select "Create My Own".**
4. **Choose "For me and my friends".**
5. **Provide a server name** and click "Create".

## Step 2: Create a New Application

1. **Navigate to the Discord Developer Portal**: [https://discord.com/developers/applications](https://discord.com/developers/applications).
2. **Click "New Application"**.
3. **Provide an application name** and click "Create".
    
    ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.07.24.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.07.24.png)
    

## Step 3: Modify Your Application

1. **Add an icon** and **description** for your application on the application's General Information page.

## Step 4: Create a Bot

1. In the left sidebar, **click "Bot"**.
2. **Click "Add Bot"** and confirm by clicking "Yes, do it!".
3. **Set bot permissions**:
    - Select **"Send Messages"** under "Bot Permissions".
        
        ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.08.19.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.08.19.png)
        

## Step 5: Enable Privileged Gateway Intents

1. Under the bot settings, find **Privileged Gateway Intents** and enable the following:
    - **Presence Intent**
    - **Server Members Intent**
    - **Message Content Intent**
    
    ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.08.02.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.08.02.png)
    
2. **Important**: Click **"Save Changes"**.

## Step 6: Generate OAuth2 URL

1. In the left sidebar, **click "OAuth2"**.
2. Select **"URL Generator"**.
3. **Select Scopes**:
    - **bot**
4. **Select Bot Permissions**:
    - **Send Messages**
    - **Read Messages/View Channels**
    
    ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.11.07.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.11.07.png)
    
5. **Copy the generated URL**.

## Step 7: Authorize Your Bot

1. Paste the copied URL into a new tab.
2. **Select the server** where you want to add the bot.
    
    ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.11.22.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.11.22.png)
    
3. Click **"Continue"** and then **"Authorize"**.

## Step 8: Obtain the Bot Token

1. Return to the **Discord Developer Portal**.
2. In the left sidebar, click **"Bot"**.
3. Click **"Reset Token"**.
    
    ![Screenshot 2024-05-19 at 18.12.17.png](../images/Discord%20Integration%20Documentation%209bfa42faffd9448b8e727c5c2d9c32cd/Screenshot_2024-05-19_at_18.12.17.png)
    
4. **Provide your password** to confirm.
5. **Save the token** for later use in your admin panel to integrate your application.

Congratulations! Your Discord bot application has been created and is ready for integration.

**Important Note: When messaging with your application, always use `!question` before any messages.**
